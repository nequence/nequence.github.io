How to Play

  • Play a card from your hand to place a chip on the matching board square.
  • Get 5 chips in a row (horizontal, vertical, diagonal) to form a sequence.
  • Two-eyed Jacks are wild — place anywhere. One-eyed Jacks remove an opponent's chip.
  • Corner squares are free for all teams.
  • First team to reach the sequence target wins!
