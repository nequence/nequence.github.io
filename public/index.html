How to Play

  • Players take turns placing chips on board squares by playing a matching card from their hand.
  • Form a sequence of 5 chips in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • Two-eyed Jacks are wild: place a chip anywhere.
  • One-eyed Jacks are saboteurs: remove an opponent's chip.
  • Corner squares are free — they count for any team's sequence.
  • First team to complete the required number of sequences wins.
